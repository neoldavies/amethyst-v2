Nail Salon FAQs – Gainsborough

Everything you need to know before your visit.

How do I book an appointment at Amethyst Nails & Beauty?
The easiest way to book is to call us on 01427 617211. We're open Monday to Friday 9:00–17:30 and Saturday 9:00–16:00. Speaking directly to our team means you get the right appointment and the right technician first time.
What is BIAB (Builder in a Bottle) and is it suitable for weak nails?
BIAB is a flexible builder gel applied directly over your natural nail to add strength and length. Our HEMA-free formula bonds without thinning the nail plate, making it ideal for weak, damaged, or bitten nails. Results typically last 3–4 weeks with zero lifting when properly applied.
How long do gel nails last, and when should I book an infill?
Gel polish typically lasts 2–3 weeks; gel enhancements and BIAB last 3–4 weeks. We recommend booking an infill appointment at 2–3 weeks to keep your nails looking their best and to protect your natural nail from lifting or snagging.
Do you offer lash and brow treatments in Gainsborough?
Yes — our beauty specialist Sorcha offers a full range of lash and brow treatments including classic and Russian Volume lash extensions, brow lamination, tinting, shaping, and lash lifts. All eye treatments are patch-tested as standard and tailored to your natural features.
Where is Amethyst Nails & Beauty located in Gainsborough?
We're located at 98 Trinity Street, Gainsborough, DN21 1HS. There is on-street parking nearby. We operate as a private studio by appointment, so please call ahead on 01427 617211 to secure your slot.
