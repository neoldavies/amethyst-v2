Why Budget Salons Cost You More

There's a reason our clients leave other salons and never go back. It's not just the results — it's what we refuse to cut corners on.

100% HEMA-Free Products

HEMA (Hydroxyethyl Methacrylate) is the cheap allergen found in most budget acrylic and gel systems. It causes contact dermatitis, nail bed separation, and permanent sensitisation. We use exclusively HEMA-free medical-grade formulations — full stop.

Anhydrous Preparation

Water swells the nail plate. Traditional wet manicures guarantee your enhancement will chip and lift as the nail shrinks back. Our dry, anhydrous prep creates a mechanical bond that locks out lifting and delivers weeks of flawless wear.

Fully Licensed Technicians

Every technician at Amethyst holds current, verifiable qualifications in their specialism. No apprentices working unsupervised. No unlicensed staff. Just trained professionals who understand nail anatomy, skin health, and safe chemistry.

Nail Health First

We won't apply an enhancement over a damaged or infected nail. We'd rather decline the booking and advise you on rehabilitation than take your money and make the problem worse. Your long-term nail health matters more than a single appointment.

Premium UK Product Lines

We invest in professional product lines — not the cheap imports stocked by budget salons. Every product we use is selected for its safety profile, performance, and longevity. Better products mean better results and healthier nails.

Personalised Aftercare

You leave with specific written aftercare instructions matched to your treatment — not a generic leaflet. We're also available on WhatsApp between appointments if you have questions, concerns, or unexpected lifting.
